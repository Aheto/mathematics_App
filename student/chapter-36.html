1. What Is Substitution?

Substitution means replacing a variable (like x, y, or p) with a given number and then computing the result.

‚úÖ Example:
If x = 2, evaluate 3x + 5
‚Üí 3(2) + 5 = 6 + 5 = 11

2. Order of Operations Reminder (BODMAS)

When evaluating, follow:

  1. Brackets
  2. Orders (powers and roots)
  3. Division and Multiplication (left to right)
  4. Addition and Subtraction (left to right)
üí° This ensures consistent, correct results.
