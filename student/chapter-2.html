2. Steps to Round Off a Number (>1,000,000,000)

  1. Identify the target place (e.g., nearest hundred-thousand).
  2. Underline that digit.
  3. Look at the digit immediately to its right.
    • If it‚Äôs 5 or more, increase the underlined digit by 1.
    • If it‚Äôs less than 5, leave the underlined digit unchanged.
  4. Replace all digits to the right with zeros.

4. Significant Figures (SF) for Large Whole Numbers

Significant figures count all non-zero digits and any zeros between them or after the first non-zero digit in a decimal context. For whole numbers without decimals, trailing zeros may or may not be significant‚Äîbut in this curriculum, we treat them as placeholders, so we count from the leftmost non-zero digit.

Example: Number = 857,386,321
Significant Figures Rounded Value

4 SF 857,400,000

1 SF 900,000,000
