5. Division and Divisibility Rules

Before dividing, check if a number is divisible using these rules:

Divisor Rule
2 Last digit is even (0,2,4,6,8)
3 Sum of digits is divisible by 3
4 Last two digits form a number divisible by 4
5 Ends in 0 or 5
6 Divisible by both 2 and 3
7 Double the last digit, subtract from the rest. If result is 0 or divisible by 7, the number is divisible by 7.
8 Last three digits divisible by 8
